How To Play:

First Player 1 shall choose either Heads or Tails and a coin shall toss landing on either heads or tails. This decides the turn order.

If Player 1 guesses correctly they shall begin rolling the dice. They do so until either they roll a 1 or they choose to hold.

At this point the other player takes their turn by rolling the dice until they roll a 1 or hold.

The turns go back and forth until someone has achieved the End Game conditions.

How To Win:

Once a player banks a total of 50 points from their dice rolls the game ends. They are declared the winner.

How to Score:

You score points subject to the number on each individual roll e.g. if you roll a 4, you get 4 points.

If you roll a 1 not only does your turn end but you lose all the points you havent banked.

To bank your points you have to hold e.g. if you roll a 6, a 5, a 3, an another 5 for a total of 19 points and used the hold feature, this would mean your banked score increases by 19. Be sure to do this before rolling a 1 in order to steadily increase your score.
